"Midnight Tales" Book Campaign

A comprehensive marketing campaign for the mystery novel "Midnight Tales" featuring billboards, posters, social media assets, and transit advertisements across major cities.

Client Riverstone Publishing
Duration 6 weeks
Role Lead Designer
Year 2024

Project Overview

Riverstone Publishing approached me to create a captivating marketing campaign for their new mystery novel "Midnight Tales" by acclaimed author J.R. Blackwood. The goal was to create a visual identity that captured the book's suspenseful atmosphere while appealing to both longtime fans and new readers.

The campaign needed to work across multiple platforms, from traditional billboards and posters to digital and social media assets. The main challenge was creating a cohesive look that would be recognizable yet adaptable to different formats and contexts.

Campaign Elements

Book Poster

Eye-catching poster design featuring the book cover and atmospheric elements.

Billboard Design

Striking visual for high-impact locations in major metropolitan areas.

Social Media Kit

Comprehensive set of assets for Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter campaigns.

Transit Ads

Designs optimized for subway, bus, and transit station placements.

Launch Event

Promotional materials for the book launch event including invitations and backdrops.

Print Ads

Magazine and newspaper advertisements in various sizes and formats.

Design Process

Research & Concept Development

I began by thoroughly reading the manuscript and conducting market research on similar book campaigns. This helped identify visual themes that would resonate with the target audience while staying true to the book's atmosphere.

Early concept sketches explored various moods and visual directions, ultimately settling on a dark, atmospheric approach with striking color accents.

Color Palette & Typography

The color palette was carefully selected to evoke mystery and intrigue while maintaining visual impact across various mediums.

Format Adaptation

One of the biggest challenges was adapting the core design to work effectively across various formats, from large-scale billboards to small social media posts.

I created a flexible design system that maintained the campaign's visual identity while optimizing for different sizes and contexts.

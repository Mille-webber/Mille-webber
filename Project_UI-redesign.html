Project Overview

As the in-house designer, I was tasked with completely redesigning our company's website to better align with our brand evolution and improve the overall user experience. The previous website had become outdated and was not effectively showcasing our products or services.

The primary goals were to modernize the visual language, improve navigation and information architecture, optimize for mobile devices, and create a more engaging user journey that would increase conversions and time on site.

Design Process

Research & Analysis

I began by conducting a thorough audit of the existing website, analyzing user flows, identifying pain points, and collecting feedback from customers and stakeholders. I also researched competitor websites and industry trends to identify best practices and opportunities for differentiation.

This research phase revealed that users found our navigation confusing, our product information scattered, and our mobile experience frustrating. These insights formed the foundation of my redesign strategy.

Wireframing & Information Architecture

Based on research findings, I restructured the site map to create more intuitive navigation. I developed low-fidelity wireframes for key pages, focusing on improving content hierarchy and creating clearer user journeys.

Multiple iterations of wireframes were tested with internal stakeholders to validate assumptions and refine the structure before moving to high-fidelity designs.

UI Design & Visual Language

I developed a refreshed visual language that maintained brand recognition while introducing a more modern, clean aesthetic. This included updating the color palette, typography system, button styles, and creating a comprehensive UI component library.

The new visual system was designed to be flexible and scalable, ensuring consistency across all pages while allowing for creative solutions to unique content requirements.

Prototyping & Testing

High-fidelity prototypes were created for key user flows and tested with real users. This phase helped identify usability issues and opportunities for improvement before development began.

Based on testing feedback, I refined the designs to address pain points and enhance the overall user experience. This iterative process was crucial for ensuring the final design would meet both user needs and business objectives.

Responsive Design

A key focus of the redesign was to create an exceptional experience across all devices. I designed with a mobile-first approach while ensuring the desktop experience took full advantage of larger screens.
